Home Remedies for Lung Diseases

1. Black Tea

Respiratory diseases get smoldered by this drink as it clears your duct to enhance passage and exit of fresh air. Drink this tea every morning and twice later on to get the boost it provides.

2. Citrus Fruits

The family of citrus fruits is richly endowed with vitamins which are effective in respiratory tract repairs and elimination of impediments on your respiratory tract. Whether you are adding water to them to consume as juice or adding a tip of sugar, they come well-fortified to boost your health and combat emphysema.

3. Exercise

Exercise helps to keep the body in an optimal state with a better circulation of blood and supply of oxygen. Indulging in regular aerobics will expedite respiratory efficiency. Begin by warming up with light-weight exercise before a lot of vigorous elbow grease. Begin with short workouts and step by step increase them.

4. Garlic

The following garlic remedy will facilitate clear congestion in your lungs throughout the first stages of the respiratory disease. Boil two or three cloves in a one-quarter cup of milk. Leave this mix it to chill, and then drink it. Do this daily to get the best results.

5. Cold Food Avoidance

Cold foods will lead to more congestion in the respiratory tracts when the processed air is trapped therein and not offloaded. It is better to avoid these foods when winter sets in so that your respiratory efficiency can be enhanced.

6. Avoid Fumes

Keep off areas where there is likely to be fumes or smoke. Since respiratory difficulties are propagated with the presence of smoke, it only comes out as a wise counsel to avoid these triggers.

7. Good Hygiene

Maintaining good hygiene keeps many infections at bay, and the easily overlooked tip is thorough hand washing. Use ordinary soap to wash your hands clean when you are back indoors and before you eat. This is vital for good health in all persons, but more importantly, for anyone already suffering from Atelectasis.

8. Camphors

Camphor is used as an effective remedy for many ailments but it works well as one of the home remedies for Atelectasis. The challenge to meet is avoiding adulterated products which are now rampant. To use camphor, mix it with water and massage to your back and chest for relief that comes almost immediately. You are sure to get relief with a few days of use and you will experience better lung movement.

9. Cinnamon and Honey

These two have properties that work well against respiratory infections and since they are easily added to warm water, they can be mixed and taken with tea or walk milk. A daily routine of this will do you lots of good. The anti-bacterial properties present in the mix makes it a worthwhile inclusion in the home remedies for Atelectasis list.

10. Ginger

Ginger is widely reviewed as a powerful anti-inflammatory, antibiotic and warming remedy for a number of health infractions. When infections lead to excessive production of mucus, ginger can clear it up and remedy the situation. Three cups of ginger daily will be effective. You can drink ginger tea or ginger ale to help with this.
